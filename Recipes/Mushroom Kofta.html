Mushroom Kofta

Description :-

  • This recipe is similar to traditional recipe but less time taking.Also,you need no cottage cheese.
    Most mushroom recipes are simple and easy.It is also lower in calories. One cup paneer roughly has 350 calories while fried mushrooms only has only 150.

Ingredients :-

For Kofta:
> 2 cup boiled potatoes.
> 7-8 mushrooms boiled until soft.
> ½ cup coriander leaves chopped.
> ½ cup breadcrumbs.
>For frying Kofta: 2 cups oil.

For gravy:
> 1 cup onion sliced.
> 1 tablespoon ginger garlic,chillies.
> 1-½ cup tomatoes chopped.
> ¼ cup cashews.
> 3 tablespoon oil for curry Adjust if reqd.

Spices:
> 1 tablespoon dhaniya or coriander powder.
> 1 teaspoon jeera or cumin powder.
> 1 teaspoon haldi or turmeric powder.
> 1 tablespoon soup stock I use vegeta or massel but this is optional.
> Salt as per taste.

Instructions :-

For Koftas:
1. Mix chopped mushrooms,potatoes and bread crumbs.
2. Add half of the spices(red chilly powder,coriander powder),salt and chopped coriander.
3. Mash well and roll it into equal sized small balls.
4. Heat oil in a wok/kadai.
5. Add the kofta balls one by one.
6. Fry them until golden brown.
7. Keep it aside.

For the curry:
1. Heat oil in a pan and add onions,ginger,garlic cloves.
2. Cover and cook for 2 minutes until soft.
3. Add chopped tomatoes and salt.
4. Cook till tomatoes soften, mixing with a spatula.
5. Add cashews to the gravy.
6. Add stock powder and mix well.
7. Cook for 5 minutes.
8. Blend the gravy with 1 cup water.
9. Heat the gravy ,along with koftas.
10. Garnish with coriander.
11. Serve with rice or roti or any flat bread.
