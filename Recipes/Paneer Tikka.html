Paneer Tikka

Description :-

  • It’s a famous Indian appetizer where pieces of paneer (Indian cottages cheese) and marinated with yogurt and spices and then grilled until charred. Onion, bell peppers and tomatoes are also added to the marination along with paneer.Once cooked, it’s served with cilantro mint chutney, onion slices and lemon wedges.

Ingredients of Paneer Tikka :-

1. 400 grams paneer.
2. 1/3 cup and 1 tablespoon cheese cubes.
3. 3/4 capsicum (green pepper).
4. 1 and 1/4 tomato.
5. 1 and 1/4 piece onion.
6. 1 and 1/3 tablespoon mustard oil.
7. 2 and 2/3 Pinches garam masala powder.
8. salt As required.
9. 2/3 teaspoon ginger paste.
10. 2/3 teaspoon kasoori methi powder.
11. 1 and 1/3 teaspoon chaat masala.
12. 1 and 1/3 cup plain greek yogurt.
13. 2/3 teaspoon red chilli powder.
14. 2/3 teaspoon garlic paste.

Steps :-

Step 1:- Cut thick paneer slices
For this easy appetiser recipe, cut paneer into large and thick slices.

Step 2:- Prepare the marinade
Prepare a marinade using all the marinade ingredients and mix well. Keep aside.

Step 3:- Marinate the veggies along with paneer
Coat the paneer slices with this marinade and add the thick tomato slices, onion and capsicum slices into the remaining marinade and marinate them as well. Keep aside for 15 minutes.

Step 4:- Garnish and serve with chutneys
Cook the tikka in Tandoor and enjoy it with onion rings and green chutney.
